# Community Launch Checklist

A complete pre-launch checklist for founders building 
structured accountability communities.

---

## 60 Days Before Launch

- [ ] Define the community's single core promise
- [ ] Write the community covenant or constitution
- [ ] Build your filtering framework (see community-filtering-framework.md)
- [ ] Set your community size limit for first cohort
- [ ] Choose your primary communication platform
- [ ] Set your pricing or entry requirement

---

## 30 Days Before Launch

- [ ] Open applications
- [ ] Begin filtering applicants through all 5 layers
- [ ] Build your onboarding sequence
- [ ] Prepare your orientation materials
- [ ] Recruit and brief any co-facilitators or coordinators
- [ ] Set up your accountability tracking system

---

## 14 Days Before Launch

- [ ] Confirm accepted participants
- [ ] Send covenant to all confirmed members for signing
- [ ] Collect any required deposits or payments
- [ ] Send orientation pack to all members
- [ ] Schedule all recurring calls for the full program duration
- [ ] Test all tools and platforms

---

## Launch Week

- [ ] Send final welcome message
- [ ] Run orientation call
- [ ] Collect signed covenants from all members
- [ ] Publish the group tracker or dashboard
- [ ] Set Week 1 deliverables
- [ ] Establish the tone immediately. Culture is set in Week 1.

---

## Post Launch (Ongoing)

- [ ] Weekly progress reviews
- [ ] Monthly culture audits
- [ ] Enforce elimination policy without exception
- [ ] Document what is working for future cohorts
- [ ] Begin waitlist for Cohort 2 at Day 30

---

## The One Rule

Launch before you are ready.
A running community you can fix is better than 
a perfect community that never starts.
